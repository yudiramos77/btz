UNIQUE FEATURES RELIABILITY & COMMITMENT

Exceptionally Trained and Rigorously Screened Uniformed Security Professionals

Bravo Two-Zero Protection will provide you with certified, licensed, insured, and bonded Security Officers. Each Security Professional has been screened to the standards of the United States Government Secret Clearance Program.

Our pre-employment background investigation far exceeds that of most police department jurisdictions. In addition, BTZP has employed a staff psychologist to oversee the initial screening of our personnel, by implementing a pre-employment written psychological inventory and a psychological interview. Furthermore, our personnel all submit to a battery of aptitude tests, a 5-panel drug screening, and extensive in-service training regarding criminal law and police defensive tactics. Prior to commencement of a contract with BTZP, we will provide you with the full applicant investigation of each proposed security officer.

We offer, free of charge, supervisory personnel to insure strict adherence to our general orders and your rules, regulations and ordinances. BTZP supervisors are utilized as a liaison between BTZP and our clients. Supervisors are on duty 24 hours a day, 365 days per year for your convenience and will regularly make spot checks to prevent the issue of complacency. It is our belief that in order to assure the best quality of service that we stand by, we must keep close relationships with our personnel both on and off duty.

At Bravo Two-Zero Protection, all of our security officers will be equipped with two-way radios or equivalent to ensure constant communication with our management team.

Each and every security officer will be in-serviced regularly regarding your specific site to further enhance the protection we provide.

Bravo Two-Zero Protection has taken a unique approach to the business of contract security. Our approach involves a proactive theory of well-groomed, exceptionally trained, uniformed security officers.
