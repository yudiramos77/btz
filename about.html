UNIQUE FEATURES RELIABILITY & COMMITMENT

Exceptionally Trained and Rigorously Screened Uniformed Security Professionals

Bravo Two Zero is committed to providing the best possible service to our clients. We are a fully licensed and insured private security and investigation agency, with over 40 years of Security, Law Enforcement and Military experience. We strive to continuously exceed our customers’ expectations. Our goal is mission accomplishment. Our greatest strength is providing excellence to our employees and clients. Our security personnel are screened, selected and highly trained professionals, ensuring our clients receive a security service second to none. We are dedicated in maintaining our reputation as a perimeter security provider by offering our clients the highest level of security available in the security industry. We sincerely believe that “good” is not good enough, and the only acceptable outcome is excellent. We train to be a proactive organization rather than a reactive one to our clients needs.

Bravo Two Zero provides security which includes armed and unarmed security officers, trained with expert capabilities to handle the most complex situations. Our commitment to creating the safest environment possible has earned us a reputation of excellence and long-term client relationships. Our management and operations team has over 45 years of operational security management ranging from: static security, mobile patrol security, executive protection, school security, industrial and construction security and security consulting. The company prides itself in delivering only the very best in quality and service with an emphasis on client expectation and satisfaction. Our expertise lies in the ability to design and implement security programs and providing security solutions for any situation. Our clients know that they can depend on Bravo Two Zero to be proactive and professional.

We are conscious that in today’s global marketplace, security is an important and vital element of any business. All of our security solutions are tailored to suit the individual security and safety needs of your business. Bravo Two Zero is there to provide peace of mind in terms of personal safety, protection and safeguarding your business practices. Therefore, our objective is to prevent risk and threat though common sense and practical solutions, with a focus on minimal to none impact to the clients business. 

Bravo Two Zero personnel reflect the highest level of professionalism that you would expect of your own employees. Overall, Bravo Two Zero provides an effective, flexible and accountable service to your business. Ultimately, the quality of security depends on the quality of the people who provide it.
